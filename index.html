About Us

Vodka And More was founded in 2007 by it's current owner Issa E. Canawati.

The Store is located in the Nativity Street, Bethlehem, a highly populated area due to the nearby stores and hotels which as a result attract many people including tourists.

Our main goal is to deliver authentic products at the lowest price possible.
